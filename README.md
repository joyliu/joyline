## What's Inside

100 curated Q&A entries organized into themes:

- **Career Navigation** - Transitions, promotions, growth paths
- **Mindset & Growth** - Burnout, resilience, learning
- **Portfolio & Interviewing** - Case studies, presentations, interview prep
- **Collaboration & Influence** - Working with PMs/engineers, stakeholder management
- **Industry & Trends** - AI, tools, market shifts
- **Design Strategy & Vision** - Roadmaps, trade-offs, defending decisions
- **Org Design & Ops** - Team structure, reviews, succession planning

Each entry follows this structure:
- **Context** - Why people ask this question
- **Reframe** - What the real question is
- **Lesson hook** - The principle or mindset shift
- **Answer** - Frameworks, examples, level-specific guidance
- **Lesson** - Explicit takeaway you can apply

## Who This Is For

- **Junior designers** navigating their first few years
- **Career changers** transitioning into design
- **Mid-level designers** trying to level up
- **Senior designers** considering leadership
- **Anyone** wondering if UX is right for them

---

## Who This Isn't For

This is mentorship guidance, not:
- Therapy or medical advice
- Professional career coaching for specific workplace situations
- Resume writing or portfolio reviews
- Promises of job outcomes
- Job referrals

---

## How to Use the Claude Skill (Pro Mode)

**5-minute setup:**

1. Sign up for [Claude Pro](https://claude.ai/upgrade) ($20/month)
2. Create a new Project in Claude
3. Copy the contents of [SKILL.md](SKILL.md) into the Project's custom instructions
4. Upload [knowledgebase.md](knowledgebase.md) as Project knowledge
5. Start asking questions!
